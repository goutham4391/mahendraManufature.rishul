Reachingthe Unreachable

How the NBFC for the nation's heart goes about its business of touching and transforming the lives of the forgotten

Scroll Down

    India was a different country in 1991, limited in its movement — and its mindset

    It wasn’t connected to the world, and in many ways, not even to itself. Movement from one city to the next could take days, and if you wanted to visit your ancestral village, north of a week. Villages and small towns were remote, and the roads that led to them kutchaa (unpaved) and ill-lit. In some of rural India’s darkest corners, drinking water was a blessing. Electricity a luxury. Mindsets were limited too when it came to money matters. Villagers were wary of outsiders, steeped in traditional modes of finance, and indebted to moneylenders and the like.

    In more ways than one, rural India was unreachable. And it was in these difficult conditions that Mahindra Finance began its business of changing lives.

The auto-rickshaw wallah. These are our treasured customers for whom we create value, and each come with their own set of struggles—and aspirations.

    Financial Freedom

    Mahindra’s pioneering spirit saw it enter this under-served rural market — long before it was fashionable, long before Rural Finance became a buzzword — in 1991 as a captive finance company that helped farmers purchase tractors. Over the years, it evolved into what it is today, an organisation with a vast reach and even greater impact. Its greatest achievement perhaps isn’t the millions it loans, but the freedom it offers. From moneylenders. From a stringent and slow system.

    And ultimately, from fear.

     

    “Majdoor se maalik (from labourer to owner) — I owe a lot to Mahindra Finance. I was an ordinary labourer working in a brick manufacturing plant. But I always wanted to start my own business of transporting goods. However, the major obstacle was getting finance to buy a tractor. I approached various banks and other financial institutions and was informed that they do not offer re-finance as a product. This is where Mahindra Finance came in and offered me a tractor loan without any hassles. Today, I’m my own boss and earning a respectable income. To be honest, Mahindra Finance has totally changed my life!- Krishna, Jhajjar, Haryana

     

     

    Inclusive Innovation

    Our understanding of our customers sees us innovate to suit not just their financial needs, but their mindset too.

    It’s why our product portfolio covers commercial, vehicles, home, SME, insurances products and services, with over multiple customised offerings. We also offer fixed deposits, mutual funds, and bonds. It’s a comprehensive bouquet that makes sure our customers are covered, no matter what their individual need is.

    Our unique Earn & Pay model leads to quicker service, flexible documentation, lower interest rates than traditional sources, and the use of assets under loan to earn from it.

    Our customer-centricity also means we offer a six month EMI instead of a monthly one.
